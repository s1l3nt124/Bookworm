Full Story of Little Red Riding Hood

Little Red Riding Hood was a sweet girl who loved to visit her grandmother. One day, she was going through the forest to visit her grandmother’s house. Along the way, she met a wolf. The wolf tricked her into telling him where her grandmother lived. The wolf went to the grandmother’s house first and pretended to be her. When Little Red Riding Hood arrived, she noticed something was wrong. With the help of a woodcutter, they saved her grandmother, and the wolf was never seen again.

Full Story of Cinderella

Cinderella was a kind girl who lived with her evil stepmother and stepsisters. They made her do all the work and treated her very badly. One day, an invitation arrived for a royal ball. Cinderella wanted to go, but her stepmother stopped her. Her fairy godmother appeared and helped her. She turned a pumpkin into a carriage and gave Cinderella a beautiful dress and glass slippers. At the ball, Cinderella met the prince, and they danced together. But when the clock struck midnight, she had to run away, leaving one of her glass slippers behind. The prince found her using the slipper, and they lived happily ever after.

Full Story of Goldilocks and the Three Bears

Goldilocks was a curious little girl who went for a walk in the forest. She found a house where three bears lived. When no one was home, she went inside. First, she tried their porridge. One was too hot, one was too cold, and one was just right, so she ate it all up. Then, she sat in their chairs. One was too big, one was too small, and one was just right, but she broke it. Finally, she tried their beds. One was too hard, one was too soft, and one was just right, so she fell asleep. When the bears came back, they were surprised to find Goldilocks, and she quickly ran away, learning a lesson to be more careful.

Full Story of Hansel and Gretel

Hansel and Gretel were a brother and sister who lived in the forest with their parents. One day, their parents took them deep into the forest and left them there. Hansel and Gretel were lost, but they found a house made of candy. Inside, there was an old witch who wanted to eat them. She trapped them in a cage, but Hansel and Gretel were clever. They tricked the witch into going into the oven and escaped. They found their way home and lived happily ever after with their father.
